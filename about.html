Our Story

From Home Kitchen to Beloved Bakery

Founded in 2018, Bangalore Bakes began as a passionate experiment in a small home kitchen. What started as weekend baking for friends and family quickly blossomed into something much bigger when people began placing orders after tasting our artisanal creations.

Our founder, Priya Nair, a former corporate professional with a lifelong passion for baking, decided to pursue her dream full-time after her sourdough bread and specialty cakes gained popularity in local farmers' markets.

Today, we're proud to have grown into one of Bangalore's most cherished artisanal bakeries, with a loyal customer base that appreciates our commitment to traditional methods, locally-sourced ingredients, and innovative flavors that blend global techniques with Indian influences.

Every product at Bangalore Bakes is still handmade with the same care and attention to detail as when we first started, using slow fermentation methods and natural ingredients that enhance flavor and create that distinctive artisanal texture we're known for.

Our Values

Quality, Tradition & Community

At Bangalore Bakes, we believe in three core values that guide everything we do:

Quality Without Compromise: We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible. Our flour comes from organic farms, our butter is churned by a family-run dairy, and our fruits are seasonal and fresh.

Honoring Tradition: We embrace time-tested baking techniques that have been passed down through generations. Our sourdough uses a starter that's been nurturing for years, and our laminated pastries take three days to make from scratch.

Building Community: We believe food brings people together. That's why we host monthly baking workshops, support local farmers, and donate unsold bread to community shelters.
