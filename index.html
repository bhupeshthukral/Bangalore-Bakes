Our Story

A Passion for Traditional Baking

Founded in 2018, Bangalore Bakes started as a small passion project in a home kitchen. Today, we're proud to be serving the finest artisanal breads and pastries in Bangalore, crafted with locally-sourced ingredients and traditional techniques passed down through generations.

Every product is handmade with care, using slow fermentation methods that enhance flavor and create that distinctive artisanal texture we're known for.
